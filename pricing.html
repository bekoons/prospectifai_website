Common questions
How does lead volume pricing work?
Active leads are contacts currently in a nurture sequence or within 30 days of completing one. Leads that have converted, unsubscribed, or been archived don't count toward your limit.
What counts as a Sprint module?
Each Sprint is a pre-built engagement arc for a specific use case — Webinar, Social, or Revival. Pro includes one Sprint of your choice. Elite includes all three. Additional Sprints can be added to any plan for $499/mo each.
Does this replace my CRM?
No. ProspectifAI sits alongside your existing CRM — HubSpot, Salesforce, or Outreach. It enriches and nurtures leads, then escalates sales-qualified contacts back into your CRM for rep follow-up.
How long does implementation take?
Starter customers are typically live within 2 weeks. Pro and Elite customers get a dedicated onboarding engineer and are usually fully operational within 4 weeks of kickoff.
What happens if I exceed my lead limit?
We'll notify you before you hit the limit and offer a prorated overage rate or a plan upgrade. We never cut off active sequences mid-nurture.
Is there a free trial?
Yes — Starter and Pro plans include a 14-day free trial with full feature access and up to 500 active leads. No credit card required to start.
Can I switch plans mid-year?
Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated. Downgrades take effect at your next renewal date.
What messaging channels are included?
Starter includes email and SMS. Pro and Elite add WhatsApp and social (LinkedIn, Instagram). All channels are managed from a single sequence editor.
