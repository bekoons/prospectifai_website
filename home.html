How it works

about your business.

Sprint modules

Four lead sources.
Four fully deployed arcs.

Start with the Sprint that matches your highest-volume lead source. Every Sprint is configured by the system from your sales collateral — no setup on your end. Add more as you grow.

Sprint Module 01

Webinar Sprint

Deploys automatically at event close within the channels your attendees consented to at registration. No post-event setup required.

  • Automatic deployment at event close
  • Email, SMS, and AI voice within consented channels
  • Pace Score qualification from your sales collateral
  • Whisper call escalation when leads are ready
  • Candor objection and competitive tracking
Sprint Module 02

Social Sprint

Captures intent from Meta comments and DMs within the 24-hour messaging window. Qualifies the lead and collects email and SMS consent before handing off to ongoing nurture.

  • Meta comment and DM intent capture
  • Qualification within the 24-hour window
  • Email and SMS consent collection
  • Handoff to email, SMS, and AI voice nurture
  • Candor capture from disengaged leads
Sprint Module 03

Revival Sprint

Re-engages dormant CRM contacts within documented consent channels. Works the leads you've already paid to generate before you spend more on new ones.

  • CRM ingestion via API or file upload
  • Deploys within attested consent channels
  • AI-built re-engagement sequences
  • Timing signal and competitive displacement triggers
  • Pace Score rebuild from responses
Sprint Module 04

Cart Sprint

Triggers on cart abandonment and re-engages within channels captured at checkout. Recovers buyers still in market and surfaces why the others didn't complete.

  • Shopify cart abandonment trigger
  • Email by default, SMS and AI voice where consented
  • Pace Score purchase intent scoring
  • Candor insight capture from non-completing buyers
  • Revenue attribution and win-back tracking
