How it works

Five steps.
Zero configuration.

01
Enrich
Every inbound lead is enriched with firmographic context and behavioral data the moment they enter the system — from your CRM, a CSV, or a live API integration.
02
Engage
Structured sequences run across email, SMS, AI voice, WhatsApp, and social — personalized to each lead's role and context, within the channels they've consented to.
03
Qualify
The AI builds qualification logic from your sales collateral and product context. Every response updates the lead's Pace Score in real time against criteria specific to your business.
04
Escalate
When a lead's Pace Score hits your threshold, escalation is automatic. Your rep receives a warm handoff with full context — or connects live via whisper call, briefed on the lead before they say hello.
05
Learn
Candor structures what non-converting leads signal — objection trends, competitive mentions, and timing patterns — automatically, across every Sprint.
Pace Score

Qualification built around
your criteria.
Not ours.

Every business qualifies leads differently. Pace Score is built from your sales collateral — call transcripts, scripts, product documentation — so the qualification logic reflects how your best reps actually sell, not a generic scoring template.

As leads respond, their Pace Score updates in real time. When it hits your threshold, escalation is automatic. Your reps only engage with leads who have already demonstrated readiness against the criteria that matter to your business.

No sequence builder. No manual threshold configuration. Your qualification logic runs at scale from day one, built by the system from the knowledge you already have.

Candor

The leads that don't convert
still tell you something.

Most platforms tell you what happened. Candor tells you why it happened. Every engagement arc — whether a lead converts or not — feeds structured intelligence back into the system.

Objection trends show you what's blocking pipeline. Competitive mentions show you who you're losing to. Timing signals show you when dormant leads are re-entering buying cycles.

Candor runs automatically across every Sprint. No manual tagging, no survey follow-up, no analyst needed. Just structured market intelligence from the engagement data you're already generating.
