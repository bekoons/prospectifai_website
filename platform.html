How it works

Five steps.
Fully automated.

01
Enrich
Every inbound lead is enriched with firmographic context and behavioral data the moment they enter the system.
02
Engage
Structured sequences run across email, SMS, WhatsApp, and social — personalized to each lead's role and context.
03
Qualify
Custom qualification questions — yours or built by our AI — score every lead against your specific criteria using Pace Score.
04
Escalate
When a lead's Pace Score hits your threshold, they're escalated to your rep. Everyone else stays in the arc until they're ready.
05
Learn
Non-converting leads surface structured intelligence — objection trends, timing signals, and competitive mentions — automatically.
Pace Score

Qualification built around
your criteria.
Not ours.

Every business qualifies leads differently. Pace Score is driven by custom qualification questions — either the ones you already use, or ones developed for your specific context by our AI.

As leads respond, their Pace Score updates in real time. When it hits your threshold, escalation is automatic. Your reps only engage with leads who have already demonstrated readiness against the criteria that matter to your business.

No generic lead scoring algorithm. No arbitrary thresholds. Just your qualification logic, running at scale.
